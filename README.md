# Mimesis-Mod-Menu

A mod menu for Mimesis with features for gameplay, player management, and visuals.

## Features

### Local Player Tab
**Protection & Safety**
- God Mode - Become invulnerable to damage
- No Fall Damage - Disable fall damage

**Recovery**
- Infinite Stamina - Never run out of stamina

### Movement Tab
**Speed**
- Speed Boost - Increase movement speed with adjustable multiplier (1x - 5x)

**Navigation**
- Teleport Forward 50u - Quick teleport forward 50 units
- Teleport Forward 100u - Quick teleport forward 100 units
- Teleport Forward 200u - Quick teleport forward 200 units

### Visual/ESP Tab
**ESP**
- Enable ESP - Toggle ESP overlay on/off
- Adjustable ESP Distance (50m - 500m)

**Visibility**
- Toggle Players visibility
- Toggle Monsters visibility
- Toggle Loot visibility
- Toggle Interactors visibility
- Toggle NPCs visibility
- Toggle Field Skills visibility
- Toggle Projectiles visibility
- Toggle Aura Skills visibility

**Visual Effects**
- Fullbright - Enhance brightness and lighting

### Inventory/Items Tab
**Item Pickup**
- Pickup All Items - Automatically collect all items on the map

**Auto Loot**
- Auto Loot Enabled - Automatically collect nearby items
- Detection Range (10m - 200m) - Adjustable range for auto looting

**Gameplay Modifiers**
- Force Buy Items - Force purchase items without cost
- Force Repair - Force repair items without cost

**Items (Requires Restart)**
- Infinite Durability - Items never break
- Infinite Price - Unlimited item prices
- Infinite Gauge - Unlimited item gauges

### Actor Tab
**Actor Management**
- View all players and monsters in a searchable list
- [P] prefix for players
- [M] prefix for monsters

**Actor Actions**
- Teleport to Actor - Teleport to selected player/monster
- Teleport Actor to Me - Bring selected actor to your location
- Kill Player - Force kill selected player
- Kill Monster - Force kill selected monster

### Misc Tab
**Mass Actions**
- Kill All Players - Instantly kill all players on the map
- Kill All Monsters - Instantly kill all monsters on the map
